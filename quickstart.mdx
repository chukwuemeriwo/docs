You can start betting on anything, against anyone, in seconds from any device or Solana-supported wallet. Follow these steps to begin using Orica right away.

## Steps to Onboard to Orica.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Visit the Orica Web App">
    First, you must visit the Orica web app at [orica.money](http://orica.money) to start betting. 

    ![image.png](/images/image.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet" stepNumber={2}>
    You’ll need to connect your wallet to use Orica. It’s our means of securely verifying your identity, managing transactions, and ensuring seamless interaction with the app.

    On the top right of the page, click the “Connect Wallet” modal and select your wallet provider. Follow the prompt and successfully connect your wallet.

    ![Screenshot2025 08 28at19 25 33 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-28at19.25.33.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're Onboarded">
    Now, you're onboarded to Orica, and you can start exploring all the features on the platform.

    ![Screenshot2025 08 28at08 13 54 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-28at08.13.54.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Bets on Orica">
    Now you can create bets on Orica. \
    Click on the `Create a Bet`   button to create a bet, and then fill in the details to make a bet.

    ![Screenshot2025 08 28at19 32 40 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-28at19.32.40.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Bets on Orica">
    You can also select and join bets on Orica.  Agree or Disagree and place your bets to have a stake in external bets and discussions.

    ![Screenshot2025 08 28at19 35 42 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-28at19.35.42.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now you can trade X (Twitter) trends and bet with or against your friends on anything.  Now, put your money where your mouth is\!
